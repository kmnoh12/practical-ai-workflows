# Creator Automation Checklist

## Workflow sentence

When __________ happens, do __________, then log __________, and notify __________ if __________.

## Trigger

- [ ] The trigger exists in the tool.
- [ ] The trigger can be tested with dummy data.
- [ ] The trigger does not require private data you do not need.

## Review gates

- [ ] Customer-facing email reviewed manually.
- [ ] Payment or delivery step tested with dummy/sandbox flow if available.
- [ ] Logs show success and failure.
- [ ] Manual fallback exists.

## First automation candidates

- Lead form to email list.
- Purchase to delivery email.
- New subscriber to welcome tag.
- Published article to repurposing checklist.
- Content idea to calendar row.
