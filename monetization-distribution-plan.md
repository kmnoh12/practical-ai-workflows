## AdSense/payment path

1. Create or use AdSense account.
2. Add site under Sites → New site.
3. Site enters `Requires review`.
4. Google reviews payments info and the entire site.
5. If approved, configure ad code / Auto ads.
6. Revenue accrues only after real traffic.
7. Later: identity/address/PIN verification as required.
8. Add tax information.
9. Add bank account/payment method.
10. Verify bank/test deposit if prompted.
11. Payment after threshold and payment schedule.

Important: bank connection is not the current blocker. Site quality and approval readiness are.
