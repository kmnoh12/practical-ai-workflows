# About Practical AI Workflows

Practical AI Workflows tests real AI tool stacks for people who work from sources: PDFs, notes, newsletters, blog posts, and digital products.

The site is built around one promise: no generic “best AI tools” lists without a workflow. Every guide should show the source, the task, the tools, the tradeoffs, and the decision.

## Who this is for

- Students and self-learners studying from PDFs and lecture notes.
- Solo creators building newsletters, digital products, and content workflows.
- Freelancers who need repeatable research, writing, and automation systems.

## What this site does not do

- It does not promise easy money from AI.
- It does not publish unreviewed mass AI content.
- It does not give legal, medical, financial, tax, or academic cheating advice.
