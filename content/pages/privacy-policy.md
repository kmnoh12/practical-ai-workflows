# Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-07-09

Practical AI Workflows is a static informational website hosted on Cloudflare Pages at `practical-ai-workflows.pages.dev`.

## Analytics

The site uses Google Analytics 4 to understand basic usage such as page views, referral sources, and download clicks. This helps decide which guides and templates to improve. Google may process this data according to its own policies.

## Downloads and links

The site offers downloadable Markdown checklists and templates. Download links may be measured as events. Some future links may be affiliate or sponsored links; those will be disclosed on the affiliate disclosure page and near relevant content when applicable.

## Personal information

The current static site does not operate user accounts, comments, payments, or a newsletter signup form. If those features are added later, this page should be updated before launch.

## Contact

For questions, use the contact page on this site. Do not send passwords, API keys, payment data, health records, or other sensitive information.

## Changes

This policy may be updated as the site adds email, affiliate, analytics, or product features.
